Vocatcher User Guide

With Vocatcher on your Mac, you can easily

  • Get definitions of words in macOS's built-in dictionary or external MDX dictionary
  • Save the words locally via shortcut keys
  • Review words
  • Manage words

Currently, only a few macOS's built-in dictionaries are compatible

  • Oxford Dictionary of English (English (UK))
  • New Oxford American Dictionary (English (US))
  • 牛津英汉汉英词典 (Chinese, Simplified-English)

There are no restrictions on external custom MDX dictionaries

Getting Started

  • Set the source of the words
  • Set up your custom hotkey in the Settings tab
  • Maybe set up your custom MDX in the Settings tab(optional)
  • Grant Screen Recording Permission when prompted

Main Interface

The app features four main tabs at the bottom.

1. Input Tab

This is where you add new words to your vocabulary.

Manual Input:
  • Type or paste a word in the text field
  • Press Return to look up the definition
  • The word is automatically saved
Quick Capture (Recommended):
  • Look up a word in macOS's built-in dictionary via three-finger gesture
  • Press your custom hotkey customized in the settings
  • The word will be automatically captured, looked up, and saved

2. Review Tab

Practice and reinforce your vocabulary using spaced repetition.

Review Interface:
  • Shows one word at a time on a card
  • Tap the card to flip it and see the definition
  • Use "Got It" or "Don't Know" buttons to rate your knowledge
Review Statistics:
  • Due Today: Number of words scheduled for review
  • Reviewed: Words you've already reviewed today
  • Accuracy: Your overall review accuracy percentage

3. Management Tab

Organize and manage your vocabulary collection.

Search and Filter:
  • Use the search bar to find specific words
  • Sort by alphabetical order (A-Z or Z-A)
  • Sort by date added (newest or oldest)
Word Actions:
  • Edit: Modify word spelling
  • Delete: Remove words from your collection
Refresh Operations:
  • Refresh the page when needed

4. Settings Tab

Customize your Vocatcher experience.

General Settings:
  • Quick Save Hotkey: Set your preferred keyboard shortcut
  • Dictionary Management: import custom MDX dictionaries
  • Add Sound Effect: Enable audio feedback for actions
  • Daily Review Goal: Set your target number of daily reviews
